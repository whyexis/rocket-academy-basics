Description

Scissors Paper Stone

The player inputs one of "scissors", "paper", or "stone", while the program randomly chooses one of the three shapes and outputs who won or if there is a draw. The rules are: scissors beats paper, paper beats stone, and stone beats scissors. If both parties choose the same object, it's a draw

Reversed Scissors Paper Stone

The rules are reversed: scissors beat stone, stone beats paper, and paper beats scissors.

Computer Scissors Paper Stone

The computer chooses a shape for the player on each turn.

Korean Scissors Paper Stone

The player or computer only wins if a draw follows after a win.

Secret Word

Guess the secret word with four hints given out after each wrong guess.

Dice Game

Guess the die roll between one to six.
